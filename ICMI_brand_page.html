Who We Are
A manufacturer built
on five generations of craft.
Indian Carpet Manufacturing Industries is an export-focused carpet manufacturer. Five generations of craft knowledge underpin every production decision we make — from material selection to the final quality check before shipment.
We operate across the full range of handmade carpet techniques, supplying importers, wholesalers, interior firms, and hospitality procurement teams across international markets.

"Craftsmanship is not a claim we make. It is a standard our looms have held for five generations."

Our Craft
Proudly
hand-crafted.
Every carpet we produce is made entirely by hand — no shortcuts, no automation, no compromise. From the first knot to the final inspection, each piece is the result of skilled craft labour refined across generations of practice.
Hand Knotted
The pinnacle of carpet craft. Each knot is individually tied by a skilled artisan — a process requiring years of training. Unmatched durability, depth, and luxury. The foundation of our export offering.
Hand Tufted
Precision tufting for high-volume contracts without compromising finish. Ideal for hospitality, commercial, and residential projects. Faster lead times, consistent output, dependable quality at scale.
Hand Loom
Traditional loom weaving delivering structural integrity and a refined surface finish. Highly adaptable across floor applications and interior specifications. A staple of our export range.
Bespoke & Custom
Full design-to-delivery customisation. Clients bring a brief — we engineer the carpet. Flat weaves, dhurries, kilims, and original constructions produced to exact specification for any market.
